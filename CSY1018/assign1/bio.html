From a young age I was interested in computers and generally in computing. I remember like yesterday the day my first personal computer arrived home and how intriguing it was. Since that day my passion for computers started to grow day by day. I recall of how much I wanted to understand the way computers work.
As I was growing up I became more and more passionate how this great invention work and realizing the variety of things it can do. Later I began exploring the Internet and I was fascinated by this whole new world.
In the following years I continued to occupy myself with computers not only as a hobby such as playing video games but also for school projects. In school I learned a few things about computing but that was just not enough, I needed an academic knowledge to fulfil my dreams.
My goal is to explore and understand the world of computing in a way that will give me the knowledge of working in this field. Especially I am interested in programming ,website development and managing databases.
Finally I choose to study in a British university because I believe the British educational system is one the best in Europe and because of the chance that I have to improve my knowledge in the English language.
